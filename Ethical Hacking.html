Ethical Hacking: A Proactive Defense

Using hacking techniques and tools to find and fix vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by malicious actors.

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the practice of using hacking techniques to improve the security of a system. The primary goal is to **identify vulnerabilities** before a malicious actor can exploit them. Ethical hackers use the same tools as cybercriminals, but their intent is to protect and not cause damage.

  • Proactive Defense: Stay ahead of attackers by finding weaknesses first.
  • Strengthen Security: Analyze and strengthen an organization's overall security posture.
  • Compliance: Ensure security measures meet industry and legal standards.
