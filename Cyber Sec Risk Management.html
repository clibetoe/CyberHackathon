Risk Assessment

Qualitative Assessment

A subjective assessment that assigns a relative probability and impact to a risk. (e.g., High, Medium, Low)

Quantitative Assessment

An objective assessment that assigns a specific monetary value to a risk. Uses the formula: **ALE = SLE x ARO**

  • **SLE** (Single Loss Expectancy): Cost of a single incident.
  • **ARO** (Annual Rate of Occurrence): How often it happens per year.
  • **ALE** (Annual Loss Expectancy): Total cost per year.
