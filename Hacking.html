Hacking & Cybersecurity: A Quick Guide

A look into the fundamental concepts of hacking, the different types of hackers, and common threat models.

What is Hacking?

Hacking is the process of exploiting system vulnerabilities and security controls to gain unauthorized access to a system's resources. The primary motivation is often to steal or redistribute intellectual property.

Advanced Threats

Advanced Persistent Threat (APT)

A sophisticated, long-term attack where an intruder maintains an undetected presence on a network to steal data over an extended period.

  • External Threats: Originates from outside the organization.
  • Stealthy: Designed to remain hidden and undetected.
  • Well-Funded: Often sponsored by nation-states.
